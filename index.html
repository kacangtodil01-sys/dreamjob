Our Story

DREAMJOB SDN BHD

Dream Job Resources Sdn Bhd was formed in September 2009, formally known as Dream Job Local Worker Agency, based in Penang. We specialise in providing Local Operators to the manufacturing industry, warehousing, logistic companies and so on.

With nearly a decade of experience, we are committed to delivering quality service and support, responsiveness to HR personnel and an experienced team to manage all manpower related activities.

Most importantly, with the tightening of the law and the increasing costs of employing foreign workers, we provide a much more cost effective solution for our clients with Local Manpower.

Got Questions?

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q1: Do you supply local manpower for industries in Malaysia?
Yes, we specialize in providing reliable and trained local manpower to various industries across Malaysia, including manufacturing, logistics, warehousing, packaging, and food processing.
Q2: What types of local workers do you provide?
We supply semi-skilled and skilled Malaysian workers suitable for factory work, warehousing, assembly lines, quality control, and other operational roles. Both short-term and long-term placements are available.
Q3: How quickly can you supply local manpower?
Depending on your requirements, we can typically provide suitable local workers within 1 to 2 weeks. For urgent needs, we maintain a pool of standby workers ready for deployment.
Q4: What industries do you serve?
We serve manufacturing plants, cold storage facilities, warehouses, logistics companies, food & beverage outlets, and retail businesses needing dependable local staff.
Q5: Do you provide transportation and accommodation for local workers?
Yes, we offer optional transportation services such as van or bus pickup to ensure punctuality. Accommodation arrangements can also be provided upon request.
Q6: How do you ensure the quality and reliability of your local workers?
All workers undergo a screening process, including background checks and interviews. We also provide ongoing supervision through on-site coordinators to maintain performance and discipline.
Q7: Do you manage payroll and compliance for local manpower?
Yes, we offer full payroll management including salary disbursement, EPF, SOCSO, and other statutory contributions, ensuring legal compliance and hassle-free administration.
Q8: Can you replace workers if they resign or are unsuitable?
Yes, we provide replacement services in accordance with our contract terms to minimize disruption to your operations.
Q9: Which locations do you cover?
Our services cover Penang, Batu Kawan, Prai, Bukit Minyak, Sungai Petani, Kulim, Alor Setar, Jitra, and surrounding areas.
